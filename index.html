The Dead Poets Society

"Dead Poets Society" depicts an unconventional English teacher who inspires his students to embrace poetry and seize the day.

Through his teachings, the boys learn to challenge
conformity and pursue their passions. However, they face
harsh consequences as they confront societal norms
and expectations.

Review



It's a heart-stirring journey. The bond between Mr. Keating and his students, their quest for self-expression through poetry, felt raw and authentic.

It's a powerful reminder to embrace our uniqueness and seize every moment. I cried.

RATING

10/10

Taare Zameen Par

A heart-touching film that delves into the journey of a young boy named Ishaan who struggles with dyslexia, feeling misunderstood and lonely in a world that fails to recognize his unique abilities.

Review


One of my favorite Bollywood movies. Made me cry so much as the main character reminded me of someone close to me. Would 100% recommend to everyone! I learned so many lessons from this film.

RATING

10/10

3 Idiots

Another heart-touching yet a comedy film about friendship, resilience, and chasing one's dreams against societal pressures. It follows the adventures of three engineering students - Rancho, Farhan, and Raju - as they navigate the challenges of academic expectations and societal norms.

Review

Again, one of my favorite Bollywood movies. This is actually my comfort movie, I watch it everytime I feel down to feel better. Again, just like the recent movies, I cried to this movie.

RATING

10/10
